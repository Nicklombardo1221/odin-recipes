RECIPE FOR LASAGNA BROTHER

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer cooked noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of noodles topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  13. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
